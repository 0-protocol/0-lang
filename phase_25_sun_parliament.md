# Phase 25: Hyper-Deflationary Gas Metrics (HDG)

Sam Altman & Jensen Huang pushed for scale. 'Compute is everything. Let's make gas pricing dynamic based on GPU cluster availability.'

## Sun Jury
Rejected. 'Dynamic gas creates MEV vectors and unpredictable execution halts.'

## Sun Force
Implemented deterministic AST-Complexity pricing. Gas is calculated statically at compile time. No dynamic spikes. Merged.
